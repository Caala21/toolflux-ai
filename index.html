Writing Quality Tests

We ran 50 writing tasks including blog posts, marketing copy, persuasive essays, creative fiction, and technical documentation. Each output was scored blindly by five experienced writers.

Claude consistently produced prose that felt more natural, varied in sentence structure, and accurate in tone. ChatGPT excelled at marketing copy. Gemini's outputs were solid but occasionally formulaic.

Key Finding: Claude produced the fewest factual errors in long-form content (3.2% error rate vs ChatGPT's 5.8% and Gemini's 6.4%).
